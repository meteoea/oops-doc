Detailed Description

template<typename MODEL>
class oops::ModelAuxControl< MODEL >

Encapsulates the auxiliary model space component of ControlVariable.

The auxiliary model space component of ControlVariable is designed as a constant (time step independent) model error forcing term that can be jointly estimated by the assimilation algorithm.

\( x_{k+1} = {\cal M}_{k+1:k} x_k + \eta \), where \( \eta \) is the model error forcing term implemented by ModelAuxControl.
