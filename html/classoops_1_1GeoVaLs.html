Detailed Description

template<typename MODEL>
class oops::GeoVaLs< MODEL >

Wrapper for geophysical values at observation locations.

Consider the \( J_o \) term of the cost function:

\( J_o = \sum ({\cal G}_k (x_k) - y_k)^T R_k^{-1} ({\cal G}_k (x_k) - y_k) \)

-> \( {\cal G}_k x_k = {\cal H}_k {\cal I}_k {\cal M}_{k:0} x_0 \); -> \( {\cal I}_k \) is the interpolation operator, such that \( g_k = {\cal I}_k x_k \);

In practice the model fields are interpolated to the observation locations during the trajectory and subsequently the observation operator is called:

\begin{eqnarray*} \sum {\cal G}_k (x_k) &=& {\cal H} \sum {\cal I}_k {\cal M}_{k:0} x_0 \\ &=& {\cal H} \sum {\cal I}_k x_k \\ &=& {\cal H} \sum g_k \\ &=& {\cal H} g \end{eqnarray*}

GeoVaLs contains interpolated model values at observation locations \( g \).
