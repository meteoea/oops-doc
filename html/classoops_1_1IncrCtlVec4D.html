Detailed Description

template<typename MODEL>
class oops::IncrCtlVec4D< MODEL >

IncrCtlVec4D class.

State part of the control vector. It has a 4D structure:

\[ dv = \begin{bmatrix} dv^0 \\ .. \\ dv^K \\ .. \\ dv^M \\ \end{bmatrix} \]

where \( dv^K \) corresponds to the K-th sub-window and is defined as:

\[ dv^K = \begin{bmatrix} dv^K_{mod} \\ dv^K_{ens} \end{bmatrix} \]

-> \( dv^K \) is implemented by IncrCtlVec
