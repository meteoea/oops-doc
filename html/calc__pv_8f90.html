Calculate potential vorticity from streamfunction.

Potential vorticity is defined as

\begin{eqnarray*} q_1 &=& \nabla^2 \psi_1 - F_1 (\psi_1 -\psi_2 ) + \beta y \\\\ q_2 &=& \nabla^2 \psi_2 - F_2 (\psi_2 -\psi_1 ) + \beta y + R_s \end{eqnarray*}
