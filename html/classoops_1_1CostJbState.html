Detailed Description

template<typename MODEL>
class oops::CostJbState< MODEL >

Pure abstract base class for the Jb term state component.

The CostJbState is the base class for the Jb term corresponding to the state part (3D or 4D) of the control variable.

Consider applying the matrix vector product:

\[ B_{state} dx_{state} = \begin{bmatrix} B_0 & 0 & .. & 0 \\ 0 & B_1 & .. & 0 \\ .. & .. & .. & .. \\ 0 & 0 & .. & B_N \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} dx_0 \\ dx_1 \\ .. \\ dx_N \\ \end{bmatrix} \]

Both \( B_{state} \), as implemented by the JbState class, and \( dx_{state} \), as implemented by Increment4D class, have in the most general case a 4D structure. In case of 3/4D-Var, CostJb3D class contains only a single component \( B_0 \) and likewise Increment4D, implemented as a vector of increments, contains only single component \( dx_0 \).
