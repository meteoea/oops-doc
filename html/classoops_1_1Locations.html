Detailed Description

template<typename MODEL>
class oops::Locations< MODEL >

Encapsulates the observation locations.

Locations of observations are extracted from ObservationSpace. Locations are passed to the interpolate and interpolateTL/AD methods of the State and Increment, respectively, in order to interpolate the model fields to observation locations (GeoVaLs).
